Hello, I'm Elena.

I believe that the objects we touch every day should ground us. My journey with clay started in the coastal cliffs of Oregon, where the raw texture of the earth first inspired me.

In 2026, my studio has evolved into a space of quiet meditation. Every mug, bowl, and vase is a result of a slow conversation between my hands and the spinning wheel. We don't just make pottery; we craft the vessels for your morning coffee, your family dinners, and your quietest moments.
