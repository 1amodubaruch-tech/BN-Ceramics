From Earth to Table

Every piece is hand-thrown in our small studio, ensuring no two items are exactly alike.

We use locally sourced stoneware clay and lead-free, food-safe glazes. Our process takes three weeks—from the initial wedge of clay to the final 2200°F firing in the kiln.

  • Sustainable: Eco-friendly packaging.
  • Durable: Dishwasher and microwave safe.
  • Unique: One-of-a-kind glaze patterns.
